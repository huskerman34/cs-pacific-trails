What is a Yurt?
Our luxury yurts are permenant structures four feet off the ground. Each yurt has canvas walls, a wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be opened.
How are the yurts furnished?
Each yurt is furnished wiht a queen-size bed with down quilt and gas-fired stove. The luxury camping experience also includes electricity and sink with hot and cold running water.Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.
What should i bring?
Bring a sense of adventure and some time to relax! Most guest aslo pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for changing weather with layers of clothing.
